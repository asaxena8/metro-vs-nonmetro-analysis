Exploring Cost of Living and Unemployment in the US

Problem Statement: How the cost of living and unemployment rates change in different parts of the United States must be understood. This will help leaders, economists, businesses and ordinary people to make good choices.

Analytical tasks:

  • Look at the correlation between the cost of living and unemployment rates in each US state.
  • After taking into account unemployment rates, identify which areas have the highest and lowest cost of living.
  • See how the cost of living and unemployment rates differ in different regions.
  • Use past data to understand how the cost of living and unemployment have been changing over time
